About me

A flight operations engineer who also writes the code

I work as a flight operations engineer, so I know firsthand the repetitive tasks that eat up hours and shouldn't: reviewing AIRAC charts change by change, manually calculating deviation points procedure by procedure.

SKYNAV came out of that frustration. Instead of accepting manual work as part of the job, I decided to build the tools I wished I had. Every application is built, tested, and used by someone who actually does this work, not just a developer outside the industry.
